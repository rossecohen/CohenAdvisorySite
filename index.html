What I Do

Growth is a systems problem.

In businesses with complex demand-to-revenue systems, demand generation and conversion are inseparable. When one part is misaligned, scaling another part usually breaks the system and increases cost faster than revenue.

I bring clarity, discipline, and accountability to how consumer businesses generate demand, convert it into revenue, and scale without amplifying waste. I diagnose how demand flows end-to-end, identify where revenue is leaking, and partner with leadership to rebuild the parts of the system that limit profitable scale.

Who I Help

Owners, CEOs, and Growth Leaders.

I work with leadership teams in consumer-facing businesses where demand does not reliably convert without orchestration across multiple functions (Marketing, Sales, Operations, Technology).

Typical Industries: Insurance brokerages, RIAs / Financial Advisors, Home Services, and other high-CAC consumer businesses.

Typical Profile: You have meaningful spend on demand generation, non-trivial conversion paths involving multiple handoffs, and ambitions to grow without accepting runaway cost or risk.

Services

How I engage.

Revenue System Diagnostic (RSD)

A rapid diagnostic designed to answer one question: What has to change so growth becomes profitable, predictable, and scalable?

What you get:

  • The Constraint Call: A clear diagnosis of what is limiting profitable growth right now.
  • The ROI Backlog: Top moves ranked by impact vs. effort.
  • Execution Plan: A sequenced 30/60/90 day plan with owners and metrics.

This identifies constraints so you don't scale demand into a system that can't absorb it.

Implementation Leadership

Partnering at the leadership level to guide execution, decision-making, and system rebuilding.

How it works:

  • Translate the diagnostic into a prioritized roadmap.
  • Make tradeoffs about sequencing, investment, and risk.
  • Guide implementation across functions (marketing, sales, ops) and vendors.
  • Maintain focus on the outcomes that matter most.

I stay involved to ensure improvements are delivered and actually stick.

Symptoms

When to call me.

If you’re spending meaningful money on growth, you may feel some version of this:

  • You increase spend, and CAC rises faster than revenue.
  • Leads are coming in, but speed-to-lead and follow-up break down.
  • Marketing and sales get stuck in an argument loop ("leads are bad" vs "follow-up is bad").
  • Dashboards look fine, but when volume increases the system gets fragile.
  • Decisions about growth are driven by anecdotes rather than evidence.
